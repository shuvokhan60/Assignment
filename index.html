About Myself

The Nutrition and Diet Center was founded in Lebanon in 1990, a pioneer in promoting a healthier diet and lifestyle, by providing balanced nutrition and calorie controlled meals. Our team of dietitians and nutrition experts strive to empower you to reach your weight goals, and achieve and maintain your long-term wellness and healthy habits. In 1996 the first Diet Center was launched in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia under the franchise of the popular AL Munajem. Al Munajem group holds a proud portfolio of Al Munajem Cold stores, Shawaya house & Najd Village restaurants, Gulf catering company, and partner of Doux, French frozen chickens in KSA.
